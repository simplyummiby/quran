About This App

Quran Reading Companion helps you follow a chosen reading plan, keep your current place, and track completed readings without pressure.

Your current reading works like a bookmark. Your completed count only includes readings you personally mark complete.

Arabic text: Hafs ʿan ʿĀṣim, using Uthmani script where available. English translation: Muhsin Khan & Taqi-ud-Din al-Hilali.

May Allah make the Qur'an a light for our hearts and accept our recitation. Āmīn.
