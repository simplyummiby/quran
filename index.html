About This Schedule

This 120-reading cycle is a working draft designed to help you move through the Qur'an steadily without tying your reading to calendar dates or streak pressure.

The plan keeps many shorter surahs whole, groups very short surahs where that feels more natural, and divides longer surahs into manageable readings that aim to follow major shifts in subject matter.

Your current reading is your bookmark. Your completed count only includes readings you personally mark complete inside this app. Starting from the middle of the schedule will not automatically mark earlier readings complete.

The divisions are intentionally provisional. As the schedule is refined, the goal is to use the natural flow of the Qur'an and trusted tafsir references, especially Tafsīr Ibn Kathīr and Tafsīr as-Saʿdī, to improve the stopping points in the longer surahs.

Arabic text: Hafs ʿan ʿĀṣim, using Uthmani script where available. English translation: Muhsin Khan & Taqi-ud-Din al-Hilali.

May Allah make the Qur'an a light for our hearts, grant us understanding, and accept our recitation. Āmīn.
